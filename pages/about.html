Who We Are

NurtureHer is a healthcare platform dedicated to supporting autistic women managing PCOD and PCOS. We combine expertise in gynecology, endocrinology, mental wellness, and nutrition to provide a holistic, user-friendly, and supportive digital space. Our focus is on simplifying symptom tracking, menstrual cycle management, and mental wellness while respecting privacy and sensory sensitivities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower autistic women with the tools and guidance they need to understand their bodies, track symptoms, and manage hormonal and mental health effectively. We strive to create an inclusive, gentle, and intuitive platform that supports well-being without adding stress or confusion.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: Calm, clear design optimized for autistic users.
  • Privacy: Health data is protected and shared only with consent.
  • Empowerment: Actionable insights and reminders to promote self-care.
  • Support: Encouraging guidance and community resources to help users feel understood.

Our Story

The NurtureHer team recognized that autistic women with PCOD and PCOS often face unique challenges in tracking cycles, symptoms, and mental health. Traditional health apps don’t always cater to sensory sensitivities or accessibility needs. We built NurtureHer to fill that gap — a safe, supportive, and intuitive platform that simplifies health tracking while promoting overall wellness.
