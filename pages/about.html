About Us

BloomCare is designed to support autistic women managing PCOD and PCOS. We understand the unique challenges faced by autistic women in tracking menstrual cycles, managing symptoms, and maintaining mental wellness.

Our goal is to provide a calm, sensory-friendly, and easy-to-use digital space where users can log their periods, symptoms, mood, and routines, receive reminders, and access helpful resources — all while respecting privacy and personal preferences.

Our Mission

To empower autistic women with PCOD/PCOS by providing a trustworthy, intuitive, and supportive health tracking app. We strive to make menstrual health and symptom management accessible, stress-free, and personalized.

Our Values

  • Accessibility: We design for autistic women with clear, calm, and simple UI.
  • Privacy: Data is private by default and shared only with consent.
  • Empowerment: Users get actionable insights and gentle reminders for self-care.
  • Support: Encouraging language, minimal distractions, and sensory-friendly design.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of healthcare professionals, UX designers, and software engineers committed to creating a tool that truly makes life easier for autistic women managing PCOD and PCOS.
